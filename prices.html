useful information for review

  • Price - This is the current price of the cryptocurrency, priced in US dollars.
  • 1h - This is the change in price over the last hour.
  • A -% means the price has gone down, a +% means the price has gone up.
  • 24h - This is the change in price over the last 24 hours.
  • 7d - This is the change in price over the last 7 days.
  • Market Cap - This is the total value of the cryptocurrency calculated by the current price x the number of tokens in circulation.
  • Volume (24h) - The is the amount of the cryptocurrency that has been traded (bought & sold) over the last 24 hours.
  • Last 7 days - This shows graphically how the token price has moved over the last 7 days.
